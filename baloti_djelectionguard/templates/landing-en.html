Baloti Voting Project

A voting platform for all residents of switzerland is our vision

ABOUT THE PROJECT

Learn and experience the swiss voting system online on baloti

With baloti, people living in Switzerland without voting rights can learn about direct democracy and try it out in practice in the form of a symbolic vote in their own national language.
